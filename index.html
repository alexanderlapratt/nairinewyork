The Atelier

Craft without
limitations.

Nairi New York is more than a bridal salon or a fashion boutique — it is one of the last bastions of a dying art: a custom Couture Atelier. From a simple hem or adjustment to the pinnacle of fashion dreams and fantasy, artistry and craft without limitations, at Nairi all tasks receive the same level of dedication and skill because our ultimate focus is on our clients and our greatest fulfillment is to help them express their best self.

Our Atelier is the creation of French-Armenian designer Nairi Barsegian, who along with her intimate, highly trained and passionate staff take dreams and translate them into fabric masterpieces. We create without ego, focusing instead on empathy to really understand our client’s needs — which is invaluable when creating bespoke, as there is no pattern, no template, only an idea that must be communicated and then created from scratch and is truly unique to each client.

We create without ego, focusing instead on empathy to really understand our client’s needs. There is no pattern, no template — only an idea that must be communicated and then created from scratch, truly unique to each client.

For some, artistry is an accident; for others it is a desire or a calling — but for Nairi it seems that artistry was destined. She is the latest in a long line of seamstresses going back many generations through her French-Armenian bloodline. Her grandmother focused her career on spinning silk into yarns to create beautiful fabrics, and her mother is a well-respected tailor who owns her own small boutique. It was in her mother’s store where Nairi discovered her unique talent at an early age — her tremendous empathy to see into someone’s soul and really understand them and their needs. This is invaluable when creating anything that is truly bespoke, as there is no pattern, no template, only an idea that must be communicated and then created from scratch.

Nairi embodies the knowledge and traditions of multiple generations of seamstresses, across multiple cultures and continents. From thread to finished Haute Couture masterpiece she has worked in each facet of the process to truly understand fashion — which is reflected in her versatility, innovative designs, and creative solutions.

Our Atelier has built a reputation for showstopping evening gowns for red carpet events and modern dramatic bridal gown masterpieces. We are recognized as a specialist in modest fashion and bridal design, and are the Atelier of choice for many traditional families for all of their special occasions. Private clients extend from New York to Miami, London, Paris, and Tel-Aviv.
