The Atelier

Craft without
limitations.

Nairi New York is more than a bridal salon or a fashion boutique. It is a custom Couture Atelier — where every task, from a simple adjustment to a haute couture masterpiece, receives the same level of dedication and skill. Our ultimate focus is on our clients, and our greatest fulfillment is to help them express their best self.

“There is no pattern, no template — only an idea that must be communicated and then created from scratch, truly unique to each client.”

— Nairi Barsegian, Designer

Our Atelier is the creation of French-Armenian designer Nairi Barsegian, whose knowledge spans multiple generations of seamstresses, across multiple cultures and continents. She trained at Oscar de la Renta and Carolina Herrera before opening her own atelier in New York’s garment district, where she and her intimate, highly trained staff translate dreams into fabric masterpieces.

Today, Nairi is recognized as a specialist in modest fashion and bridal design — the atelier of choice for many traditional families for red carpet eveningwear, modern dramatic bridal gowns, and every special occasion in between. Private clients extend from New York to Miami, London, Paris, and Tel-Aviv.
